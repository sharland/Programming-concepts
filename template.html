How to use this slide-deck

This slide-deck uses either space or the right arrow to proceed and the left arrow to go back. You can also use the controls in the bottom right-hand corner.

Certain slides will have an additional arrow in the controls pointing down. This means there are sub-slides underneath the main slides

If you wish to see an overview of all the slides tap escape on your keyboard.
